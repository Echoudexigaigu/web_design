Major Figures of Wei

The state of Wei was shaped by powerful leaders and brilliant strategists whose political and military influence lasted well beyond the Three Kingdoms era. Here are the three most iconic figures associated with Wei.

Cao Cao (曹操)

Cao Cao was the architect of Wei’s rise and one of the most capable rulers of the period. Known for his strategic genius, political pragmatism, and administrative reforms, he unified northern China and laid the foundation for Wei’s power.

Zhang Liao (张辽)

One of Wei’s most formidable generals, Zhang Liao earned lasting fame for his defense of Hefei, where he repelled overwhelming Wu forces. His discipline and battlefield insight made him a pillar of Wei’s military strength.

Sima Yi (司马懿)

Sima Yi was a brilliant strategist and influential statesman whose political maneuvering eventually enabled his family to replace the Cao lineage. His victory over Zhuge Liang and later consolidation of power reshaped the future of Wei and China.
